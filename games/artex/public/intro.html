The experiment is divided in two parts: Preparation and Game.

Part 1: Preparation

In Part 1 you will not interact with other players, and you cannot earn points unless esplicitely mentioned in the screen. Part 1 involves three steps:
  • First, some basic psychological and demographics characteristics will be collected.
  • Secondly, the instructions of the game played in Part 2 will be shown to you, followed by a short understanding quiz.
  • Finally, you will play in practice mode to get familiar with the game interface.

Part 2: Game

In this part you will interact with other human participants. You might need to wait until enough participants are connected, but the waiting time should be short and up to just a few minutes.
  • The game will be repeated for a certain number of rounds, and each round you will have a chance to earn points based on your actions and decisions.
  • After the game, you will be shown a questionnaire consisting in a short compulsory part followed by a series of optional questions.

Payout scheme

The total sum of points collected during the experiment will be transformed in USD with the exchange rate 1000 : 1 and paid to you as a bonus.
