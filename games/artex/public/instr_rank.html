Introduction

You are part of a community of 9 painters (including you). You will play the role of both the artist and the art critic. The community will collectively decide which paintings can be displayed in 3 artistic exhibitions: A, B, and C.

Always keep in mind: In this game you will act both as an artist and as an art critic.

The game

The game is repeated rounds. Each round is divided in 4 steps:
  • Step 1: Each participant creates 1 painting.
  • Step 2: Each participant chooses 1 exhibition to which to submit the work of art.
  • Step 3: Each participant reviews 3 of the 8 paintings produced by the other artists. The review is done by assigning a score on a scale from 0 to 10.
  • Step 4: The best paintings are displayed in the exhibition to which they have been submitted, and generate reward points for their authors. Paintings that are not displayed never generate reward points. The complete payoff scheme is explained below.

Who reviews what

  • At each round you will be randomly assigned to review 3 paintings, and you can never review your own painting.

Other rules

  • You have a limited amount of time to complete each step of the game. The time left will be displayed on the screen.
  • At each new round, when creating a new painting, you will continue from your previous submission.
  • It is possible to copy paintings from the past exhibitions.
